Problem: Which choice does the Greedy algorithm always make in a given problem situation?
A. Worst choice
B. A random choice
C. The optimal choice
D. The first choice
Correct Answer: C
Incorrect Answer: B

Problem: Dynamic programming is most effective for problems with which characteristic?
A. Overlapping subproblems
B. Non-overlapping subproblems
C. Independent subproblems
D. All subproblems
Correct Answer: A
Incorrect Answer: B

Problem: In which way does the backtracking algorithm solve the problem?
A. Bottom-up approach
B. Top-down approach
C. Both
D. Neither
Correct Answer: B
Incorrect Answer: A
